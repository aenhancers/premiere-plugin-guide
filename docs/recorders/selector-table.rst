Selector Table

| ``recmod_Close``                | PrivateData      | unused                           |

| ``recmod_SetActive``            | PrivateData      | ``(csSDK_int32) boolean toggle`` |
+---------------------------------+------------------+----------------------------------+
| ``recmod_SetDisp``              | PrivateData      | ``recDisplayPos``                |
+---------------------------------+------------------+----------------------------------+
| ``recmod_Idle``                 | PrivateData      | ``recGetTimecodeRec``            |
